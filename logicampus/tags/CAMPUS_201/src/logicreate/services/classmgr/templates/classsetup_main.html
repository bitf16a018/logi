Choose which style you wish to apply to your students classroom view

Select a "style" for your classroom below. Choosing the style below will alter the color theme of your classroom. This will help students and yourself easily identify which class they are in by the theme you choose below. We suggest to not change this once the class is setup as it may confuse students once they are used to viewing your classroom in a certain style. Once you make a change to the style, you must log out in order to view the change to your classroom.
