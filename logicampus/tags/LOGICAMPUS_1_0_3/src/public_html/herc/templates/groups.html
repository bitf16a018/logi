Groups...

By creating multiple groups for your site you can regulate which information, content, or areas users can access. Access to groups are defined as permissions: read, write, update, delete, and approve. By selecting various permissions on modules and assigning users to various groups you control access to your content and your site.

Permissions...
To control permissions on a group, highlight the desired group and press "set permissions". Basic allow/deny access to each module can be done be turning off 'read'. Other permissions are module specific and may not apply to all modules.

Create a new group...
Give your new group a description so you can easily remember what purposes it serves. Descriptions can be up to 32 characters. Next, in the "Group ID" space enter a name or code for your group. This name can be any numbers or letters up to eight characters.

Delete a group...
Select the desired group and press "delete" at the top. You will be asked to confirm this delete. Special system required groups cannot be deleted. Users are not deleted when their group is deleted.
